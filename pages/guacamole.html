Homemade Guacamole

Descriptions

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip, spread, or salad first developed in Mexico. In addition to its use in modern Mexican cuisine, it has become part of international and American cuisine as a dip, condiment and salad ingredient.

Ingredients

  • 2 avocaods, peeled and pitted
  • 1 cup chooped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 jalepeno pepper, seeded and minced(Optional)
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl until creamy
  2. Mix tomatoes, onions, cilantro, lemon juice, and jalapeno pepper into mashed avocado until well combined; season with salt and black pepper.
